About

Modeling worlds, one vertex at a time.

I'm Vaenir, a 3D modeler and artist who loves turning concept art into finished, game-ready assets — everything from characters to full environments, though my main focus is game props: mugs, bowls, tents, the little things that make a world feel lived-in. Most pieces start out in Maya or Blender, where I block out the model and keep tweaking it detail by detail until it feels right.

Once a model is locked in, I move on to texturing, mostly hand-painting in Procreate and Photoshop, with Substance Painter and Sampler in the mix for more material-driven work. I try to give every piece the best I've got, and I'm always happy to pick up new tools and techniques along the way.
